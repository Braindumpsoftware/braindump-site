Privacy Policy

Last updated:

BrainDump helps you capture thoughts, brainstorm with AI, and convert ideas into tasks. We collect only what’s needed to run the app, improve reliability, and support subscriptions.

What We Collect

  • Email address: For account sign‑in and support.
  • User ID: An internal identifier to manage your account and sync.
  • Purchase history: Subscription status and receipts to provide premium access.
  • User content: Brain dumps and tasks that you create. Stored locally and, if you sign in and enable sync, in our database.
  • Usage analytics: High‑level product interaction (e.g., screens used) to improve the app.
  • Crash data: Diagnostics that help us fix issues.
  • Device ID: A device‑level identifier used only for analytics/diagnostics (not for ads).
  • AI processing: Text you send to the AI is transmitted to OpenAI to generate a response.

What We Don’t Collect/Do

  • No third‑party advertising SDKs.
  • No cross‑app tracking or sale of data.
  • No precise location or contacts.
  • Audio from speech‑to‑text is not stored by us.

Voice Input

If you use voice‑to‑text, the app requests microphone and speech recognition access. Audio is not stored by us; only the transcribed text you choose to save or send to AI is handled.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we store preferences on‑device and schedule local notifications. No notification content is sent to our servers.

Third Parties

  • Supabase: Authentication and database storage for sync.
  • RevenueCat: In‑app purchases and subscription status.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Product interaction analytics and crash diagnostics.
  • OpenAI: Processes text you submit to AI features to generate responses.
  • Apple: Speech services and distribution (App Store).

Retention and Control

We retain data while your account is active. Local data stays on your device until you delete it. If you enabled sync, you can request account and data deletion and we will remove your data from our systems and processors within a reasonable period.

Security

Data in transit uses HTTPS. Avoid entering sensitive personal information into prompts.

Children

BrainDump is not directed to children; we require a parent/guardian to manage any under‑13 use consistent with local law.

Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be reflected by the date above.

Contact & Deletion

Email: support@braindumpapp.org. To request account/data deletion, contact us at this address and include the email used for your account.
