Privacy Policy

Last updated:

BrainDump helps you capture thoughts, brainstorm with AI, and convert ideas into tasks. We minimize data collection and respect your privacy.

What We Collect

  • User Content (Local): Brain dumps, tasks, and BrainBuddy messages are stored on your device using local storage.
  • Optional Cloud Sync: If you enable Supabase syncing, your data is transmitted to and stored on your Supabase project.
  • AI Processing: Text you submit to AI features is sent to DeepSeek solely to generate responses.

What We Don’t Collect

  • No required account by default.
  • No analytics or ads tracking for MVP.
  • No precise location.

Voice Input

If you use voice-to-text, the app requests microphone and speech recognition access. Audio is not stored by us; only the text you choose to keep or send to AI is handled.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we store preferences on-device and schedule local notifications. No notification content is sent to our servers.

Third Parties

  • DeepSeek (AI): Processes submitted text to generate responses.
  • Supabase (optional): Stores synced data if you enable sync.
  • Apple/Google: Provide speech services and distribution.

Retention and Control

Local data stays on your device until you delete it. Use “Clear All Data” in the app to remove local data. If you enabled Supabase sync, you can delete synced data via your account or by contacting us.

Security

We use HTTPS for AI requests. Avoid entering sensitive personal information into prompts.

Children

BrainDump is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be reflected by the date above.
