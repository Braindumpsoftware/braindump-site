Privacy Policy

Last updated:

BrainDump helps you capture thoughts, brainstorm with AI, and convert ideas into tasks. We minimize data collection and respect your privacy.

What We Collect

  • Contact Information: Your email address for account creation and login (via Supabase Authentication).
  • Identifiers: An internal user ID (Supabase) and an app instance ID (Firebase). We do not link Firebase data to your identity.
  • Usage Data (Analytics): Aggregated app interactions and screen views to understand feature usage (Firebase Analytics). No advertising features or cross‑app tracking.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data to keep the app reliable (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • User Content: Brain dumps, tasks, and BrainBuddy messages you create. These are stored on your device (local storage) and, if you sign in, synced to Supabase for your account.
  • AI Processing: Text you submit to AI features is sent to DeepSeek solely to generate responses.

What We Don’t Collect

  • No precise location.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA) and no cross‑app tracking.

Voice Input

If you use voice-to-text, the app requests microphone and speech recognition access. Audio is not stored by us; only the text you choose to keep or send to AI is handled.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we store preferences on-device and schedule local notifications. No notification content is sent to our servers.

Third Parties

  • Supabase (Authentication & Database): Stores your account and any data you choose to sync.
  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): Provides aggregated analytics (screens, events) and diagnostics (crash/performance reports). We do not enable advertising features.
  • DeepSeek (AI): Processes submitted text to generate responses.
  • Apple/Google: Provide speech services (if used) and app distribution.

Retention and Control

Local data stays on your device until you delete the app or remove it. If you sign in and sync to Supabase, you can delete your synced data at any time using the in‑app Delete Account feature (Profile → Delete Account). This permanently removes your account and associated synced data.

Security

We use HTTPS for all network requests. Avoid entering sensitive personal information into prompts.

Children

BrainDump is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be reflected by the date above.
